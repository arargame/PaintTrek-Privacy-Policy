# Paint Trek – Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 2025**

Paint Trek respects your privacy. This game does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

---

## **Information We Collect**
Paint Trek does **not** collect any personal data, including but not limited to:

- Name, email address, phone number  
- Device ID  
- Location data  
- Photos, media, or files  
- Contacts  
- Microphone or camera data  
- Advertising identifiers  

No personal data is gathered, stored, or transmitted.

---

## **Crash Logs and Diagnostic Data**
The game may use Google Play’s built-in crash reporting system.

These reports are:
- Anonymous  
- Contain no personal or identifying information  
- Used only to improve game stability  

Paint Trek does not store or manage any crash logs manually.

---

## **In-App Purchases**
If the game includes optional paid features, purchases are processed **exclusively through Google Play Billing**.

- We do **not** receive or store credit card data.
- All payment information is handled securely by Google.

---

## **Third-Party Services**
This game does **not** integrate:
- Advertising SDKs  
- Analytics SDKs  
- Tracking libraries  

No user data is shared with third parties.

---

## **Children's Privacy**
Paint Trek does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

---

## **Changes to This Policy**
We may update this policy occasionally. Updates will be published in this repository.
